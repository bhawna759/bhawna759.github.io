About

A product thinker, grounded in data and the customer.

I'm a data and analytics leader who has spent my career owning products end-to-end — sitting between business, engineering, and data-science teams to turn unclear, high-stakes problems into solutions people actually use.

Today I'm a Manager, Data Analytics at Optum (UnitedHealth Group), where I drive delivery of BI and analytics products for US healthcare Providers and Payers. I shape requirements, define the KPIs that matter to each stakeholder group, run delivery through Agile and Scrum, and track success the only way that counts — revenue, efficiency, adoption, and time-to-market.

I keep the customer at the centre of every decision and I'm comfortable saying what I can and can't yet do — I'd rather be precise than impressive. I'm grounded in modern AI through formal certification and apply it pragmatically inside production analytics products, while continuing to deepen my product-tooling craft.
